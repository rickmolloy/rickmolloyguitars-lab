Lab ethos

ToneLab is built for builders: clear units, realistic assumptions, and validation against real plates—not theoretical ideals. No black boxes, no “trust me,” and no claims beyond what the data will support.

This work stands on the shoulders of Trevor Gore’s acoustic modeling and is shaped by the teaching spirit of Robbie O’Brien: practical, repeatable, and accessible.
